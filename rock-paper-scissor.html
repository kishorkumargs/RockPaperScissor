Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors!

Rock Paper Scissors is a fun and interactive web-based game built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Play against the computer and test your luck in this digital version of the timeless hand game!

  • ✊ Rock beats Scissors
  • ✋ Paper beats Rock
  • ✌️ Scissors beats Paper

Each win gives you +3 points, a tie gives you +1 point, and a loss deducts 1 points.
Rank is determined by your total points:

  • Newbie (0-9)
  • Beginner (10-29)
  • Intermediate (30-49)
  • Pro (50+)

Try to reach the Pro rank!
